How it works

Made to feel human on both sides of care.

For the person taking photos, it feels guided and approachable. For the clinician reviewing the case, it arrives with a cleaner story, better context, and less missing information.

01

Start with what you can see.

Take a few photos of the affected area with just enough guidance to get useful images, without turning the moment into a technical task.

02

Answer what actually matters.

A short set of questions adds the missing context around symptoms, timing, and concern, so the case feels understood before anyone even opens it.

03

Move the case forward without the usual friction.

Screening details and captured images can be routed onward for clinician review, helping teams respond faster and helping people feel less stuck in uncertainty.

Why it helps

Less friction before the consult. More clarity when the case arrives.

PelliScope is designed for real first-contact moments: at home, at a front desk, inside an outreach workflow, or anywhere a skin concern needs a gentler and faster start.

For people at home

It offers a calmer way to act on a concern instead of waiting, guessing, or abandoning the process halfway through.

For clinics and intake teams

It reduces some of the repetitive back-and-forth that usually happens before a case is ready to review.

For clinicians

It helps cases arrive with better structure, a clearer sense of urgency, and a smoother path into the review queue.
